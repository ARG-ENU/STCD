Bus journeys
More and more people are turning to the bus to find cheaper, quicker and greener transport – and to avoid the frustration of being stuck behind the wheel!  Whatever your reason for thinking of switching it’s an excellent time to get on the bus.  Bus use in Nottingham is already the highest in the country, outside London, and it has increased by a fantastic 3% in the past year.
