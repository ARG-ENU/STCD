Travel by tram
Trams are quick, easy and convenient.  With trams running every 12 minutes at peak times you’ll never have to wait long to get on board.

Route planning – Tram
Combine your tram journey with walking, the bus or a short walk to get you to your destination.

Tram tickets
Choose from a range of ticket types - season tickets, day passes, leisure deals and Kangaroo.

Tram stops
Park at any of the 5 Park & Ride sites free of charge when you use the tram.
