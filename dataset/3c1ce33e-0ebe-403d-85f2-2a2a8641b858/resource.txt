Staff Travel to Work Loan scheme

Furthermore, the University is offering salaried employees an interest-free loan towards the purchase of an annual travel card. A loan of up to £2,000 is available to purchase a travel card for the purpose of commuting to work.  The loan is repaid via 10 monthly salary deductions.
