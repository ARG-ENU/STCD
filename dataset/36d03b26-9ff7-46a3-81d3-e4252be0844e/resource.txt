Travel by train
There are loads of  rail stations in Nottingham and the surrounding areas which make it easy to travel by train.  With local rail stations such as Alfreton, Attenborough, Beeston, Burton Joyce, Carlton, Hucknall, Newark North Gate, Netherfield, and East Midlands Parkway – it’s easier than ever to catch the train for a local or national journey. By walking or cycling to the station you can improve your health whilst saving the environment at the same time! Find out more about the facilities at Nottinghamshire’s stations.
